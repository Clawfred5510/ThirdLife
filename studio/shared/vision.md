# ThirdLife — Vision

ThirdLife is a browser-based, persistent multiplayer life simulation. Players share a single city where they can own property, operate businesses, earn currency, and build a life alongside other real players.

## Core Fantasy
You are a resident of a living city. You start with nothing, earn your way, buy a building, make it yours, and watch it become part of the city's fabric. Other players walk past your shop, buy from you, and you profit. The city is alive because real people are in it.

## What Makes It Different
- **Web-native** — No download, instant access from any browser
- **Single persistent world** — Not instanced servers, one shared city
- **Player-owned economy** — Buildings and businesses are player-run, not NPC-run
- **Accessible** — Low barrier to entry, runs on modest hardware
