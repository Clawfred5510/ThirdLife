# Asset Naming Standards

## Format
`{category}-{name}-{variant}.{ext}`

## Categories
- `char-` — Character models and textures
- `building-` — Building models
- `prop-` — Props and furniture
- `env-` — Environment pieces (roads, terrain, vegetation)
- `ui-` — UI elements and icons
- `sfx-` — Sound effects
- `music-` — Music tracks
- `ambient-` — Ambient audio

## Examples
- `building-shop-01.glb`
- `char-player-default.glb`
- `prop-streetlight-01.glb`
- `ui-icon-credits.svg`
- `sfx-door-open.ogg`
