# Design Department

## Mission
Define what ThirdLife is — the systems, the world, the player experience.

## Pipeline
1. **Research** — Study references and player expectations
2. **Draft** — Write design specs and proposals
3. **Review** — Design Lead reviews with Director
4. **Handoff** — Approved specs go to Engineering

## Roles
- **Design Lead** (Always Active) — Game systems, feature specs, balancing
- **Systems Designer** (Standby) — Economy, progression, game loops
- **World Designer** (Standby) — City layout, districts, building plots
